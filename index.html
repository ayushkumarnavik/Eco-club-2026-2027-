ABOUT ECO CLUB

At DAV Public School, NIT Campus, Adityapur, joining as an Eco Member means becoming part of a dedicated community committed to environmental protection and sustainability. The Eco Club operates under the guidance of teachers and student representatives to promote a greener, cleaner and healthier environment.

OBJECTIVES

  • Promote environmental awareness.
  • Develop eco-friendly habits.
  • Encourage tree plantation.
  • Protect biodiversity.
  • Reduce pollution.
  • Promote sustainability.
  • Create responsible citizens.
  • Conserve water and energy.

CAMPUS GREENING

Regular plantation drives are organized and gardens are maintained to improve greenery throughout the campus. Students actively participate in preserving nature and enhancing biodiversity.

AWARENESS PROGRAMS

Rallies, seminars, poster-making competitions, debates and environmental campaigns are conducted to educate students regarding waste reduction, climate change and conservation.

CLEANLINESS DRIVES

The Eco Club actively supports Swachh Bharat initiatives and cleanliness drives with SUPW. Special programs are conducted for maintaining clean classrooms and surroundings.

WASTE MANAGEMENT

Single-use plastics are discouraged. Segregation of waste and recycling are promoted. The "Cleanest Class" reward program motivates students to maintain cleanliness.

SCHOOL OVERVIEW

DAV Public School NIT Campus is located inside National Institute of Technology, Jamshedpur. The school functions under the aegis of DAV College Managing Committee and strives for academic excellence and holistic development.
